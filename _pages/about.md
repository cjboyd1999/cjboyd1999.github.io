So, who is "Cameron Boyd"? Well, I'm currently a Cyber Security Analyst by title, but my role sees me touching a mix of incident response, risk assessment and security sales. I thoroughly enjoy what I do as I'm always presented with a challenge to overcome. My top interests within security include penetration testing, incident investigation and consulting.

As with most IT roles, constant education and upskilling is the key to making yourself a better expert. My professional journey started back in 2018, when I started a Bachelor of Cyber Security at Deakin University. Throughout my degree, I was able to meet and work with some great people, who I can only hope I'll get another chance to work with in the future. In my final year, I had the ever-common "impostor syndrome", where I wasn't sure how I compared to professionals in the industry. I realised going above and beyond my studies would afford me the extra confidence that I was looking for.

In 2020, I undertook and completed the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) course and became certified after successfully demonstrating my expertise in the exam. In late 2020, I finished up my university studies and graduated with a Bachelor of Cyber Security with Distinction. With these two big accomplishments in 2020, I felt that I was ready to begin my career.

Since starting in my current role, I have managed incidents ranging from simple business email compromises to organisation-wide ransomware attacks. As the first formal cyber security employee, my role also involved creating relationships with vendors which has allowed me to work with some of the biggest companies within the cyber security sector.
